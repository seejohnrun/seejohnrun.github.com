KinderType

KinderType teaches kids to identify a letter, hear it, find it on the keyboard, and spell over 500 of the most common and useful vocabulary words. Perfect for learning to read, spell, and type!


Privacy Policy

KinderType does not story any personal data about users or their behavior. The app is entirely self-contained on your device, and makes no requests (tracking or otherwise) with any external service.
